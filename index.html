⛳ Golf Majors Pool — About & Disclaimer
What is this?

This is a private dashboard for the annual Golf Majors Pool — a friendly competition among invited participants across five major golf championships each season: The Players Championship, The Masters, the PGA Championship, the US Open, and The Open Championship.

The pool is operated for entertainment purposes only among a private group of known participants. Entry fees and payouts are social in nature and are not offered to the general public.

Data & Accuracy

Historical standings, scores, and payout data are sourced from official pool records and are believed to be accurate. Player pick data reflects submitted entries as recorded.

Live scoring data (when enabled) is sourced from the DataGolf API and reflects real-time tournament information. Tournament odds shown in the Preview tab are for informational reference only and sourced from publicly available markets — they are not offered as betting advice.

Venue information, course details, and tournament facts for future events are based on publicly available sources and confirmed R&A, USGA, and PGA of America announcements. Some future event details (dates, yardage, setup) are subject to change.

Not Gambling / Legal

This pool is a private, social competition among friends and acquaintances. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated through any licensed gambling or gaming operator.

Participants are responsible for understanding and complying with any applicable laws in their jurisdiction regarding informal pools and competitions. This dashboard does not facilitate wagering, does not accept payment, and does not operate as a gaming platform.

Privacy

This dashboard is accessible only to invited pool participants via a private link. Entry names and scores are shared only within the pool group. No personal data is collected, stored, or transmitted by this dashboard — all data is embedded directly in the page and processed entirely in your browser.

Please do not share the dashboard link publicly or with anyone outside the pool.

Not Affiliated

This dashboard is an independent fan project. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to the PGA Tour, Augusta National Golf Club, the PGA of America, the USGA, the R&A, DataGolf, Splash Sports, RunYourPool, or any official golf governing body or tournament organizer.

All tournament names, course names, and related marks are the property of their respective owners and are referenced here for informational purposes only.

Contact

Questions about the pool, picks, or payouts? Reach out to the pool commissioner directly. This dashboard is maintained privately and is not monitored for external inquiries.
